LEMON MERINGUE PIE

One 9-inch pie

Preparation Time : 30 minutes

Cooking time : 10 minutes

Servings : 8

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 1/2 cup(s) water
  • 2 teaspoon(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup(s) white sugar
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked

DIRECTIONS

  1. Step 1:

    • preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)

  2. Step 2:

    • Make lemon Filing: In a medium saucepan...
    • whisk together 1 cup sugar,flour,cornstarch and salt
    • stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest
    • Cook over medium-high heat until mixture comes to a boil
    • stir in butter
    • place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture
    • bring to a boil and continue to cook while stiring constantly until thick
    • remove from heat
    • pour filling into baked pastry shell

  3. Step 3:

    • whip eggs whhites until foamy
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form
    • Spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust

  4. Step 4:

    • Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes or until meringue is golden brown
